Welcome to the Simba Classroom!

The Simba classroom is a nurturing and stimulating environment for our infants. Our curriculum is designed to foster their earliest developments through sensory exploration, language exposure, and responsive care.

Curriculum Agenda

  • **Sensory Exploration:** Activities designed to stimulate the five senses. Examples include tummy time on various textures, exploring soft blocks, and listening to a variety of sounds and music.
  • **Language Development:** Caregivers engage in constant conversation, singing, and reading to the children. We use books with different textures and high-contrast images to encourage focus and attention.
  • **Gross and Fine Motor Skills:** We provide safe spaces for babies to practice rolling, sitting, crawling, and eventually walking. Fine motor skills are encouraged through reaching for toys and grasping objects.
  • **Social-Emotional Growth:** Our focus is on building a secure attachment through warm, responsive, and consistent care. We respond to the babyâ€™s cues to build trust and a sense of safety.
