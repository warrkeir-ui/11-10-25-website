Enrollment at Hakuna Matatah

We are excited that you are considering Hakuna Matatah for your family! Our enrollment process is designed to be as smooth as possible. We encourage you to schedule a tour to meet our staff and see our classrooms in action.

Enrollment Procedure

  1. **Contact Us:** Reach out to us via our contact page, phone, or email to express your interest and schedule a tour.
  2. **Visit Our Center:** We will give you a tour of our facilities and you will have the opportunity to meet our teachers and ask any questions you may have.
  3. **Complete the Packet:** If you decide to enroll, please complete our enrollment packet. You can download the packet below.
  4. **Submit Documents:** Return the completed packet along with all required documentation to the center director.
  5. **Welcome to the Family:** Once your enrollment is approved, we will confirm your childâ€™s start date and welcome you to the Hakuna Matatah family!

Fee Schedule and Policies

Tuition payments are due on Mondays at the start of each week. Billing will be done on Tuesday, and late fees will be added if tuition has not been paid in full.

  • A late fee of $35.00 will be charged on Tuesday if tuition has not been received.
  • An additional $50.00 will be added if tuition has not been paid by Wednesday.
  • A late charge of $5 per minute will be applied if children are not picked up at the contracted time.
  • Payments are accepted via cash, debit card, and checks.
  • Returned checks will be charged a $35.00 NSF fee, and we will no longer be able to accept payments via check.
