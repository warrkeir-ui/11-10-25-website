Welcome to the Pumba Classroom!

The Pumba classroom is our preschool program, focused on preparing children for kindergarten. Our curriculum is built around hands-on, multi-sensory learning that encourages critical thinking, literacy, and numeracy skills in a fun and engaging way.

Curriculum Agenda

  • **Literacy and Language:** We introduce letters, sounds, and sight words through interactive phonics games, story-building activities, and writing centers. We encourage children to tell their own stories and express ideas.
  • **Math and Science:** Our curriculum uses manipulatives to teach early math concepts like counting, sorting, patterns, and basic addition. Science lessons involve hands-on experiments and observations about the world around them.
  • **Creative Arts:** Children express themselves through painting, drawing, sculpting, and music. We provide opportunities for dramatic play and imaginative thinking.
  • **Social Skills and Self-Help:** We focus on developing important social skills like cooperation, conflict resolution, and empathy. Children are encouraged to take on classroom responsibilities to build independence.
