Welcome to the Timon Classroom!

The Timon classroom is a vibrant and engaging space for our toddlers. Our curriculum is designed to encourage their growing independence and curiosity through hands-on learning, social interaction, and creative exploration.

Curriculum Agenda

  • **Exploratory Play:** We provide a variety of materials and centers for children to explore freely, such as a water table, sand table, and art stations.
  • **Language Skills:** We build on foundational language with interactive story time, singing songs with hand motions, and simple, guided conversations to expand vocabulary.
  • **Problem-Solving:** Activities are designed to challenge toddlers, such as shape sorters, simple puzzles, and stacking blocks. We encourage them to think and solve problems on their own.
  • **Social-Emotional Development:** Toddlers learn to interact with peers through cooperative play, sharing toys, and learning to express their feelings with words. We focus on building empathy and self-regulation skills.
